What should this site feel like to the people using it?

This shapes the visual design, tone, and which features get top billing on day one.

A

Driver & Owner-Operator Community

Feels like a trusted peer resource — practical, no-nonsense, built for the solo driver and small owner-operator. Heavy focus on daily brief, community pulse, load boards, parking, fuel, rates, and road-level issues.

Think: TruckersReport meets a modern news dashboard.

B

Small Fleet Business Intelligence Tool

Feels like an operations dashboard — structured, data-forward, built for the owner who manages 2–25 trucks. Heavy focus on compliance calendar, safety scores, driver hiring, insurance renewal prep, and fleet cost tracking.

Think: A lightweight command center for small fleet operators.

C

Trucking Insurance & Compliance Intelligence Hub

Feels like a specialized research and alert tool — focused on regulatory changes, safety score impact, insurance cost drivers, and compliance deadlines. Differentiated by depth on the insurance and FMCSA side.

Think: A vertical intelligence product for operators who care about risk and cost.

D

All-in-One Trucking Operating Hub (Broad)

Covers all three audiences equally — drivers, small fleets, and compliance-focused operators. The homepage adapts by topic, not persona. Best for building the widest audience fast, but harder to feel deeply specialized.

Think: The Bloomberg of small trucking businesses.
